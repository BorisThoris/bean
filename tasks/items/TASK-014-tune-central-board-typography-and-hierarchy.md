# TASK-014: Tune central board typography and hierarchy

## Goal

Make the central board communicate phase, timer, leader, and next action at a glance.

## Scope

Review text sizes, labels, ordering, contrast, and update frequency.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Phase and timer are the most prominent board elements.
- Leader and mode details are readable but secondary.
- Board text does not look like debug output.

This can be physical text in the world, not Razor UI.
